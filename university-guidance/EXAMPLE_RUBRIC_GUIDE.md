# University Guidance Setup

Place your university-specific files in this directory:

## Required files

- RUBRIC_PRIORITY_MAP.md — Your module's marking criteria and grade bands
- CANVAS_SUMMARY.md — Key requirements from your module's Canvas/VLE page

## Evidence levels (used by Rubric Evidence Gate)

When citing rubric requirements, always label the evidence level:

- OFFICIAL ORIGINAL TEXT — Exact wording from official university documents
- LOCAL CANVAS SUMMARY — Your summary of Canvas/VLE content
- INFERENCE FROM LOCAL SUMMARY — Your interpretation (flag as TO CONFIRM)
- EVIDENCE INSUFFICIENT — Cannot locate the requirement

## Notes

- Do not commit official university documents to a public repository
- Keep only your working summaries here
- Mark any unverified requirements as TO CONFIRM
