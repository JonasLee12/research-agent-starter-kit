# Writing Style Trajectory

Status: `CONTEXTUAL / STYLE GUIDANCE`

## Direction

First meet the project's actual requirements. Then borrow useful journal-style discipline where it helps:

- clear problem statement
- evidence-led argument
- concise contribution
- transparent methodology
- careful limitations
- implications that match the evidence

## Style Levels

1. Clear research-project writing.
2. Strong requirement-aligned project output.
3. Journal-informed academic prose.

## Guardrail

Do not let journal-style polish override project requirements, evidence boundaries, ethics rules, or supervisor/PI/client/reviewer guidance.
