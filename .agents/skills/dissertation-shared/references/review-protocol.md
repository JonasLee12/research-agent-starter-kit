# Review Protocol

Use an independent reviewer stance.

Check:

- Are the research questions aligned with title, literature gap, method, findings, and discussion?
- Is the theoretical framing explicit and appropriate?
- Does the co-design method answer the stated questions?
- Are claims supported by participant evidence or literature?
- Are limitations stated honestly?
- Are ethical and adoption concerns handled responsibly?

Output findings first, then recommendations. Separate confirmed issues from suggestions.
