# Viva Prep

Use near submission or defense preparation.

## Workflow

1. Read dissertation summary, chapter plans, findings, and limitations.
2. Generate likely questions by category:
   - motivation and contribution
   - active learning theory
   - AI-agent design choices
   - co-design method
   - participant sampling and ethics
   - analysis validity
   - findings and implications
   - limitations and future work
3. Draft concise answers with evidence references.
4. Identify weak spots and prepare honest responses.
5. Create presentation outline if requested.

## Outputs

Create or update:

- `viva/LIKELY_QUESTIONS.md`
- `viva/ANSWER_BANK.md`
- `viva/TALK_OUTLINE.md`

## Guardrails

Do not overclaim. Make limitations sound rigorous, not defensive.
