## Gap type

Choose one:

- Empirical gap — a population, context, or phenomenon not yet studied
- Methodological gap — existing methods have limitations that a different approach could address
- Theoretical gap — current theories do not adequately explain the phenomenon
- Contextual gap — findings from one setting have not been tested in another
- Integration gap — separate bodies of literature have not been connected
